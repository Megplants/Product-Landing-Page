What is Seeding Square?

Seeding Square is an amazing, color-coded seed spacer that organizes and optimizes vegetable gardens!

Make your seed planting experience fast, easy, and more accurate!

Identify and remove weeds more easily when your seeds are planted in a uniform grid.

Double or even triple your harvest by optimizing your planting space!

Features

  • Simplifies the planting process with color-coded spacers that correspond with the Planting Guide.
  • Planting Guide that optimizes spacing for each plant type. Color-coded chart has a complete list of standard garden vegetables with easy to follow instructions.
  • Leaves perimeter impressions in the soil so you can see where your seeds are planted.
  • Magnatized Wand/Spoon with depth indicators for making holes in the soil and handling seeds with ease! When you are finished the magnet attaches the wand securely to the Seeding Square so it's ready for next time.
  • Funnel that fits into the holes, making sure your seeds go where you want them to. Funnel attaches to the Seeding Square for safe keeping when not in use.
  • Small profile for easy storage.
